Welcome to the Memory Game

You can read about the rules here: Rules

Rules:

  1. The game consists of a deck of cards with identical pairs of numbers, shuffled and placed face down on a playing surface.
  2. The player's objective is to flip over pairs of matching cards by selecting two cards at a time.
  3. The game starts with all cards facing down.
  4. On each turn, the player flips over two cards.
  5. If the two cards match, they remain face up and the player scores points.
  6. If the two cards do not match, they are flipped back over and the player tries again.
  7. The game continues until all pairs have been matched and all cards are face up.
  8. The player's score is determined by the number of turns it took to match all the pairs.
  9. The goal is to complete the game with the fewest number of turns possible.
